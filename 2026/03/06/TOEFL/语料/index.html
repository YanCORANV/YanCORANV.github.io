1. 投诉、建议与解决 (投诉与建议场景)

  • 问题描述：I am writing to bring your attention to an issue regarding…
  • 程度词：Fell short of expectation (未达预期) / Pose a safety hazard (构成安全隐患)
  • 行动建议：I would strongly suggest that you consider…
  • 补救措施：Take steps to rectify it (采取措施纠正) / Address this issue promptly (迅速处理)

2. 致歉、请假与补救 (致歉与解释/学术沟通场景)

  • 正式道歉：I sincerely apologize for…
  • 说明原因：Due to a sudden medical emergency / Encountered an unforeseen technical issue
  • 承认疏忽：Administrative oversight (行政疏忽)
  • 补救行动：Catch up on the missed material (赶上进度) / Review the meeting materials or recording
  • 后续跟进：Attach the completed draft and happy to make any necessary revisions
  • 承诺：I assure you this will not happen again

3. 申请、自荐与合作 (申请与自荐/社交协作场景)

  • 意向表达：Formally apply for… / I am enthusiastic about this opportunity
  • 个人优势：My skills align with your needs / Be a valuable asset to your team
  • 背景描述：As a student with a passion for… / Have previous experience in…
  • 态度与承诺：Dedication to… / Eager to contribute my skills to ensure the event runs smoothly
  • 协作词汇：Propose a new initiative (提出新倡议) / Collaborate on… (在…方面协作) / Brainstorm ideas

4. 通知与变动 (通知与安排变动场景)

  • 正式告知：I am writing to notify you of… / Please be advised that…
  • 解释变动：Due to an unavoidable schedule conflict / Change of venue (场地变更)
  • 确认与感谢：Please confirm if this new time slot works for you / Appreciate your flexibility

5. 随性社交 (非正式场景)

  • 开场与约饭：I’m reaching out to… / Grab some burgers at the new place downtown
  • 叙旧与求助：Catch up / Hang out / Do me a favor
  • 变动与感谢：Take a rain check (改天再约) / I owe you one (欠个人情) / Count me in! (算一个)

1. 逻辑框架 (Template Fragments)

  • 表达立场：From my perspective, the [A] is better.
  • 回应与补充：Apart from the [理由] of [具体点] that [同学名字] points out/notes, another point I want to add is that…
  • 解释与举例：In other words… For instance…
  • 让步转折：Admittedly, as [同学名字] notes, nevertheless, [新观点].
  • 总结：In conclusion.

2. 教育与成长 (Education)

  • 核心词：Holistic development (全面发展) , Well-rounded individual (全面发展的人) , Civic responsibility (社会责任) , Academic burden (学业负担) , Interpersonal skills (人际能力) , Privilege for the wealthy (富人的特权)
  • 论据：
    • Sth fosters critical thinking and collaboration far more effective than [A].
    • Prepare them for the emotional and social complexities of modern life.
    • Ensures that education remains a way for hardworking students, not a privilege for the wealthy.
  • 万能案例：In my high school, we had a mandatory conflict resolution workshop where we practiced active listening and empathy during disagreements. The training proved more useful than calculus theorems I learned.

3. 科技与生活 (Technology)

  • 核心词：Indispensable (不可或缺) , Instant access to sth (即时获取) , Media literacy (媒介素养) , Discern true info from digital fabrication (辨别真伪)
  • 论据：
    • Technology should serve as a tool to enhance human ability, not a substitute for irreplaceable physical experiences.
    • The digitalization of daily tasks, while [A], risks eroding the traditional social fabrics that rely on face-to-face interaction.
    • In an era of information overload, media literacy is key.
  • 万能案例：
    • AI： [AI outputs] are grammatically perfect but lack my unique voice and critical insights. Relying on AI can stifle the intellectual growth that university education is supposed to foster.
    • 深度伪造： I once shared a story only to find out it was deepfake; we must cross-reference with reputable, peer-reviewed sources.
    • 虚拟旅游： During a high-definition virtual tour, I missed the ambient whispers and the sheer scale, which are sensations only physical travel can provide.

4. 环境与社会 (Environment)

  • 核心表达：
    • Mitigate urban congestion and environmental degradation (缓解拥堵与恶化).
    • Hold polluters accountable (追究污染者责任).
